About Us

The Dublin fitness scene has been completely revolutionised over the past few years with a record number of people in the capital taking up dedicated health and wellness regimes. With new spaces opening all the time, from chic changing facilities to world-class trainers, interesting class variations and delicious post-workout fuel, it has never been so easy to get fit in the capital. Plus, becoming healthier is no longer about punishing ourselves for our supposed indulgences. For the best results, your workout regime should make you feel better too. Our goal at Feat Hub is to connect you with the best personal trainer, nutritionist, and to give our member daily tips on healthy diet, workout plan, and lifestyle that are dedicated to building your resilience, in accordance with modern sports science theory.
